Network Administrator

Reference number: 00125

Salary: $195,000 +

Company Location: 57 ValeTech Avenue, 3rd Floor, Nova Park Business District, Riverton, CA 90231, Victoria

Work Mode Available: Hybrid

Solvex is seeking a dedicated Network Administrator to join our growing team. In this role, you'll be responsible for managing and maintaining the company's internal networks, ensuring a secure, stable, and high-performing infrastructure. You'll work closely with developers, designers, and leadership to support daily operations and implement IT solutions that drive efficiency and innovation.

If successful, you will report to IT Manager or Head of IT / Infrastructure.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor, maintain, and optimize company networks and servers
  • Configure and manage routers, switches, firewalls, and wireless access points
  • Troubleshoot and resolve network-related issues in a timely manner
  • Manage system backups, software updates, and security patches
  • Implement and maintain network security protocols (firewalls, VPNs, antivirus, etc.)
  • Control user access and permissions
  • Document network infrastructure, configurations, and procedures
  • Provide technical support and guidance to internal teams
  • Assist with IT planning, upgrades, and expansion projects

About you

What we require Preferred
  1. Solid understanding of networking concepts (TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, etc.)
  2. Experience with configuring and maintaining networking hardware
  3. Knowledge of cybersecurity best practices
  4. Ability to diagnose and resolve technical issues independently
  5. Degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field (or equivalent experience)
  • 2+ years of experience in network or systems administration
  • Hands-on experience with both Windows and Linux environments
  • Familiarity with cloud services (e.g., AWS, Azure)
  • Experience with remote access tools and VPN configuration
  • Industry certifications (e.g., CompTIA Network+, Cisco CCNA, MCSA)
